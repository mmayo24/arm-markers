Memorial Monument Services Monument Sales We offer individualized consultation services to discuss your new memorial stone needs and we offer memorial design, lettering and installation services. Cemetery Lettering At Acton Monument, we can add names and dates to your existing monument. We take great pride in matching your existing monuments fonts and type size. With your lettering order, we visit the cemetery to get rubbings and measurements. Then, we mail you an acknowledgement with a price quote. Monument Cleaning We offer memorial stone cleaning where we use the appropriate cleaning solution and power washing when necessary. In order to provide you with an accurate memorial cleaning price, we will visit the memorial site and then mail you a price quote for cleaning the memorial. Concrete Foundations All upright monuments require footings and most cemeteries have strict requirements to follow. Not all cemeteries provide foundation services. If a cemetery does not offer foundation services, Acton Monument can handle this for you and we will work this service into our price quote.
