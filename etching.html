HAND ETCHED ARTWORK & PORTRAITS
Classic and elegant, hand etched images are by far the most realistic artistic memorial portraits that can be acheived on a headstone. Hand diamond etched images have a more lifelike quality than do machine diamond etched images, and are far superior to laser etching a portrait or image - which does not deeply impact the stone in the same way that hand diamond etching does.
Specialized diamond-plated tools and skilled artistry is used. For best results, hand etched memorial portraits should be at least 6" tall. A high quality photo is needed to achieve an accurate likeness - the better the quality of the photo submitted, the better the final result of the portrait will be.

Hand etchings are a versatile art form that adds a customized aesthetic touch to your memorial. We use modern techniques to hand etch beautiful artistic scenes and designs onto your granite memorial including wildlife art, portraits, emblems, logos and much more that depict the life, loves, hobbies or occupation of your loved one. The only limit to what we can do is your imagination.

Can etching be done on any color granite? To achieve the very fine detail, and to maximize the enduring crispness and clarity, we prefer not to etch colored, gray or white granite monuments. Etched designs are recommended to be made on black granite. Our etching technique is permanent. Designs, pictures, scenes and portraits may be either plain or color-etched We can use photos of most size and subject matter: from single and family portraits to panoramic ranch and farm scenes

IMAGE RESOLUTION
When it comes to etching the first rule of thumb is: Input Quality equals Output Quality. Many families submit photos and images whose resolution or actual physical size handicaps the quality of the final etched image. The best etchings have both high resolution and are of a large size. Should you have further questions, please feel free to speak with our art department.

Etching is a method used to transfer more detailed images onto granite.
The image can be a portrait of your loved one, a nature scene or anything that will personalize your memorial of distinction. We first create a freehand drawing to scale which is transferred to the headstone. We then precision etch the image by hand directly into the polished surface. We can even add color to the etching (touch color).
