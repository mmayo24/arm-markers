Why are they tough enough for a cemetery? It is an advanced high resolution digital image transfer technology with a custom designed, computer controlled, high temperature kiln firing process. This transfer technology is NOT DYE SUBLIMATION it is a kiln fired, non-toxic, environmentally friendly process that guarantees high standards in terms of image quality, clarity and durability. All images are fired at very high temperatures (1650 °F, 900 °C) making the finished product very durable. It is recessed into a granite base for a rock solid finish and is ready to last into perpetuity. It easily withstands the abuse and weight of cemetery lawn mowers.

FULL-COLOR MARKERS are durable and resistant to:
  • Extreme Hot or cold climates will not crack.
  • Ultra Violet-Rays -guaranteed not to fade.
  • Graffiti can easily be removed.
  • Will not scratch from lawn mowers or foot traffic.
  • Wind nor rain can cause damage.
  • Chemicals can not penetrate the surface
